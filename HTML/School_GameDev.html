Cosmic Undead

Game Overview

It's a thrilling RPG adventure multiple platformer game. Elves, humans, and creatures of fantasy have reached the outer corners of space, where science and adventure run free. But every rose has its thorn, meaning a massive pandemic of undead. Our main hero hunts space zombies in spaceship wreckages with a smile. She's not the brightest bulb, but hey, she's got bills to pay.

The main character hunts space zombies in spaceship wreckages with a smile. I want to emulate a silly character who loves floating around while killing the undead. She is unbothered by her job and the creatures she kills.
